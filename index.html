0-2000 = Novice

2000-4000 = Advanced Beginner

4000-6000 = Intermediate

6000-8000 = Expert

8000-10000 = Pro

10000 Above = Master

10000 HOURS

10000 Hours is an app that helps you track your progress to mastery. The top right corner will show your skill level based on how many hours you have logged into the tracker. The progress bar on the left tracks your hours up to 100 and then goes back down to zero.

Rinse, grind, and repeat.

Who said making it to the top was ever easy?



Hotkeys Control


Press "N" to log at new task.


Press "U" to scroll to the top of the page.


Press "D" to scroll to the bottom of the page.
